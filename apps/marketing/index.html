Features

Everything you need to run operations with confidence

From daily checklists to compliance training — built for managers who need visibility, not more spreadsheets.

Task assignment

Assign tasks to individuals or entire departments. Fan out work instantly and track status per person.

Escalating reminders

Automated reminders before due dates, overdue alerts for staff, and escalation to owners when work slips.

Department dashboards

See completion rates, overdue work, and team progress by location and department at a glance.

SOP & training management

Attach documents and SOPs to tasks. Staff acknowledge they've read and understood — managers see who hasn't.

Require photo, document, or signature proof when tasks are marked complete. Evidence stays attached to the assignment.

Audit trail

Every assignment, status change, and acknowledgement is logged. Export reports when you need a paper trail.
